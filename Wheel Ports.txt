For the robot's wheels, the output ports are arranged as follows:
Header: F = Front, M = Middle, B = Back
Subs: 	T = Top, B = Bottom
		D = Drive, A = Articulation

Note: 	Right Middle does not work on box side. Most likely a problem with the Sabertooth.

Left:
F:
	D: BM
	A: BL
M:
	D: TR
	A: TM
B:
	D: TL
	A: BR

Right:
F:
	D: BR
	A: TR
M:
	D:
	A: 
B:
	D: BM
	A: TM
